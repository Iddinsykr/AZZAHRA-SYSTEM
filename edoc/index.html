ABOUT US

Klinik AzZahra, located in the heart of Pekan, Pahang, was established with the mission of providing high-quality healthcare services to the local community. Since its opening in May 2023, the clinic has grown to offer a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, chronic pain management, minor surgeries, eye and ear cleaning, medical check-ups, and more. The clinic’s dedication to accessible, affordable, and patient-centered care has made it a trusted healthcare provider in the area. With extended operating hours from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. every day, Klinik AzZahra is committed to meeting the evolving healthcare needs of the Pekan community.


The clinic has also embraced modern healthcare practices by adopting advanced medical technologies, enhancing the efficiency of patient care. However, recognizing the need for an organized and effective system to manage patient records and appointment scheduling, Klinik AzZahra has implemented a new Registration and Appointment System. This system is designed to streamline administrative processes, reduce wait times, and improve patient satisfaction, ensuring that every patient receives prompt and efficient care. With a growing team of skilled medical professionals, Klinik AzZahra continues to innovate and provide exceptional healthcare services to the community.
